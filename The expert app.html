The Expert App

UX Case study of an expert pairing app

Project Overview

he goal of this app is to give people a simple, intuitive way to connect with an expert in nearly any field within seconds so they can feel more informed and more prepared to face their everyday (and not-so-everyday) problems.

The Problem

Our users need a way to access experts in order get their questions answered in a time of need. We will know this to be true when we see users effectively completing their personal projects with expert advice.

Research Goals

To better understand user behavior and learning To determine which tasks users would like to complete while using a learning app / site. Documenting user pain points with existing learning apps / sites.

Discovery

To begin the discovery process I surveyed 6 participants in order to better understand user behavior when seeking expert advice. I documented user pain points with existing expert apps on the market and collected data on the context In which users would use the expert app.

User Journeys

The goal of this user flow was to identify friction points and opportunities within user flows and design solutions to fix them. I wanted to ensure that the user flow benefited the users and the business with data driven results. The goals were improving usability, gaining retention, creating conversion, reducing costs and Improving accessibility.

User Personas

My user personas are a direct reflection of our research efforts. I conducted user interviews, contextual inquiries, and competitive analyses to gather a comprehensive dataset of user needs, goals, frustrations, and behaviors.

Key Insights

  1. Users seek expert advice frequently ranging from once a week to a few times a week.
  2. Users recognize how invaluable expert advice is. Seeking expert advice is an integral part of their lives and how they navigate challenges
  3. Users are looking for good communication skills, approachability, someone who has extensive experience and a proven track record in their respective field
  4. Users prefer a phone call with the expert they’ve paired with and want a chat feature when seeking answers quickly.

Affinity Maps

After conducting comprehensive tests, I strategically opted to employ an affinity map to discern valuable insights from the data. This method facilitated the isolation and interrelation of diverse pieces of information, enabling a clear visualization that unveils previously unnoticed patterns and hierarchies.

What's Next

As I reflect on the journey of crafting the UX research portfolio for "The Expert App," I’m inspired by the insights gained and the potential for growth in this project. Looking ahead, my focus shifts towards enhancing the user interface (UI) of the app. Building upon the foundations laid by the research findings, I am committed to refining the visual elements, interactions, and overall design to create a seamless and delightful user experience.

Conclusion

As we wrap up our exploration of "The Expert App," the blend of heartfelt stories and hard-hitting data points a complete picture of user experiences. From the genuine empathy of qualitative insights to the precision-guided decisions of quantitative data, we're poised for design upgrades. This portfolio isn't just a record; it's a glimpse into the future. Paired with real stories and reliable data, I’m set to enhance "The Expert App". Every click and tap will be more than a feature—it'll be a mini-adventure in a user-friendly, empowering, and enjoyable app. Thanks for joining us on this journey of discovery and growth!
